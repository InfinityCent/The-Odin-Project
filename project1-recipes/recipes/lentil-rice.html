Lentil Rice

Description

Lentil Rice is a very common Persian dish that is often cooked at home. It consists of rice mixed with lentils, along with other ingredients such as ground beef and/or chicken, various spices, and raisins. It is delicious when eaten with yoghurt. Here, we will cook lentil rice with added ground beef.

Ingredients (4 portions)

  • 3 cups of white basmati rice
  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 300 grams ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • turmeric powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • canola oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix 6 teaspoons of salt with 3 cups of white basmati rice and soak in water for at least 2 hours.
  2. Soak lentils in water for at least 2 hours.
  3. Chop 1 large onion and fry over some canola oil until they have turned golden.
  4. Add raw ground beef to the onion and using a spatula, work on separating it into smaller chunks.
  5. When the beef has turned slightly brown, add 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Mix.
  6. Continue cooking beef until it has fully turned brown. Set aside.
  7. Get a separate pot and fill it with water, put it to boil.
  8. Pour the rice into a sieve and briefly rinse it, then add boiling water. Wait about 10 minutes until the rice has softened (but do not wait so long that it has gone fully soft).
  9. Pour out the boiling water and rice into a sieve again, then add a significant amount of oil to the bottom of the pot used for rice (this will make the cooked rice crispy and delicious - called tahdig).
  10. Pour out the lentil into a sieve and rinse, then add to the rice.
  11. Add the cooked beef to rice, along with one cup of water along the edge of the pot. Put on the lid (wrapped in a towel first) and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes.
  12. Mix all the ingredients in the pot and serve. Enjoy!
